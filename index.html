ABOUT US

DN Healthcare & Nutrition is a new age Ayurvedic & Nutraceutical modern research-based company devoted to the development of natural medicines (herbal products/nutraceutical) of the highest quality, with experience that is backed up by rigorous scientific research, at its own manufacturing facility at Gurgaon and Dehradun, India with top-class quality control and R&D activities.

DN Healthcare & Nutrition combines the time-honored wisdom of traditional knowledge with modern and ancient science of Ayurveda and presents to the world affordable and effective products free from side effects and heavy metals and pesticides.

Vision

DN Healthcare & Nutrition will be a research-driven, world-class nutraceutical and naturals supplement company, providing premium quality (nutraceutical product/natural herbal products and services).

Mission

DN Healthcare & Nutrition is to carry out scientific research on all available nature-based herbals/nutraceutical products, both traditional & non-traditional, make marketable products from them and market the most reliable and safe products at the most affordable prices possible.

  • To provide top-quality nutraceutical & herbal products for the promotion of good health.
  • To utilize the most advanced technologies and incorporate cGMP standards for product research, development and manufacturing.
  • To blend our values and aspirations through innovative, healthy and encouraging work environment.
  • To promote ecologically sustainable development including use of organic medical plants.
  • To create team spirit within the organization and its entire network.

Responsibility

We always monitor scientific reports on all environmental issues and on the reliability and safety of our products and take corrective action whenever and wherever necessary.

All production processes and design techniques are formulated with the condition of minimizing water consumption and environment pollution.
